## 🌋 The Problem at Planetary Scale

The Earth is failing in four simultaneous, interconnected crises — and every existing response system treats them as separate problems.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                      THE FOUR CONVERGING CRISES                         │
│                                                                         │
│  🌿 BIODIVERSITY COLLAPSE    │  🌾 FOOD SYSTEM FRAGILITY               │
│  ─────────────────────────   │  ─────────────────────────               │

│  75% of Earth's land altered │  Climate reduces yields 2-6%/decade      │

│  Tipping points cascading    │  Communities bear 100% of the cost        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**The deeper problem is not data. It is not technology. It is three structural failures:**

1. **No accountability layer** — Polluters, deforesters, and bad actors operate with impunity because there is no tamper-proof, real-time record of what they are doing to ecosystems.

2. **No economic alignment** — Communities who live inside and around ecosystems receive no financial benefit from protecting them. The economic incentive runs 100% toward extraction.

3. **No connected intelligence** — Satellites collect data. Sensors collect data. NGOs collect data. None of it is connected into a system that can *reason across all of it simultaneously*, issue real-time alerts, deploy physical responses, and pay out automatically to the people who take action.

**EDEN exists to close all three gaps simultaneously.**

**Why Mamba-2, not a Transformer?**

Standard Transformers scale quadratically with sequence length — processing a decade of daily satellite data for every 10m pixel in Kenya would require compute that doesn't exist. Mamba's selective state space mechanism maintains linear complexity with sequence length, which means EDEN can hold *decades* of continuous ecological memory per region — remembering seasonal patterns, migration corridors, and historical interventions without running out of context. For planetary-scale environmental data, this is not an aesthetic choice. It is the only architecture that works.

**Build sequence — fine-tune, never pre-train from scratch:**
```
Month  1–6  : XGBoost ensemble (NDVI + rainfall + soil → maize yield). Classical ML first.
Month  6–12 : Fine-tune Mamba-2 on Sentinel-2 East Africa time series (10,000 GPU-hrs ≈ $20K)
Month 12–24 : Deploy MoE with 4 expert modules → scale to 8 → 16 → 32
Month 18–36 : World Model v0.1 — cross-pillar causal reasoning (food × climate × biodiversity)
```

STRATEGIST-α ranks threat, proposes response (ranger dispatch vs. drone overfly)
   ▼  < 30 seconds
ETHICS-0 validates (FPIC clear, action lawful in jurisdiction)
   ▼  < 60 seconds
Ranger receives GPS-precise SMS alert with audio hash evidence link
   ▼  < 5 minutes from sound to boots on ground coordination
CHAIN-NOTARY logs entire sequence — immutable legal evidence

## 🔑 The Three Decisions That Determine Everything

> These are not engineering decisions. They are strategic decisions. Get them wrong and no amount of technical sophistication saves the project.

**1. Start with farmers, not with technology.**
Find 200 farmers in Nakuru County with a real yield prediction problem. Understand exactly what information changes their planting decisions. Build the minimal system that delivers that information reliably. Then expand. The technology serves the farmer — the farmer does not serve the technology demonstration.

**2. Prove accuracy publicly before asking anyone to bet real money on it.**
Every EDEN model runs in shadow mode — publishing predictions without financial consequences — until it has a publicly verifiable accuracy record of at least 80% over 6 months. Only then does real money enter the contracts that depend on that model's output. This is what converts sceptics into believers.

**3. Make community members owners, not data sources.**
Mau Forest communities who receive carbon credit revenue defend the forest because it is their income. Farmers who receive automatic M-Pesa insurance payouts promote EDEN to their neighbours. Rangers who earn $EDEN for confirmed alerts check their phones constantly. Economic alignment is the most powerful adoption mechanism available — and it is already built into the architecture.
